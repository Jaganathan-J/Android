### Product Requirements Document (PRD)

#### Product Overview
TaskFlow is a next-generation task management application designed to streamline productivity for individuals and teams. The app leverages modern Android technologies to deliver an intuitive, fast, and secure experience.

#### Problem Statement
Traditional task management apps often suffer from poor UI/UX, outdated architecture, and insufficient collaboration features. TaskFlow addresses these pain points by integrating real-time updates, AI-driven insights, and seamless integrations.

#### Value Proposition
TaskFlow enhances productivity by providing a unified platform for task creation, tracking, and collaboration. Its advanced features include:
- Real-time task synchronization
- AI-powered task prioritization
- Voice commands for quick actions
- Cross-platform compatibility

#### User Personas
1. **Individual Users**: Busy professionals needing to manage personal tasks efficiently.
2. **Team Leaders**: Managers requiring team task coordination and progress tracking.
3. **Developers**: Tech-savvy users seeking a robust, customizable solution.
4. **Students**: Students managing academic deadlines and projects.

#### Core Features (MoSCoW)
**Must Have**: Task creation/editing, due date reminders, progress tracking.
**Should Have**: Collaboration features, voice commands, analytics dashboards.
**Could Have**: AI recommendations, integrations with other tools (e.g., Google Calendar).
**Won't Have**: Overly complex workflows, non-essential gamification elements.

#### Functional Requirements
1. **Task Management**:
   - Create/Edit tasks
   - Set due dates and priorities
   - Track progress percentage
2. **Collaboration**:
   - Share tasks with team members
   - Assign tasks to specific users
3. **Notifications**:
   - Push notifications for overdue tasks
   - Email/SMS alerts
4. **Data Sync**:
   - Real-time sync across devices
   - Offline mode with auto-sync
5. **User Auth**:
   - Sign up/login via Google/Apple
   - Role-based access control

#### Non-Functional Requirements
1. **Performance**: Tasks should sync within 2 seconds of creation/modification.
2. **Security**: All data transmissions must use TLS 1.3 or higher.
3. **Usability**: Intuitive UI with minimal onboarding required.
4. **Compliance**: GDPR and CCPA compliance for user data.

#### Success Metrics (KPIs/OKRs)
- User Acquisition Rate: 50% monthly growth in the first 6 months.
- Task Completion Rate: Average of 80% per user.
- App Store Rating: Maintain 4.5+ stars on Google Play Store.

#### Rollout Plan
1. **Alpha Testing**: Q2 2025 (Internal team and select users).
2. **Beta Testing**: Q3 2025 (1000+ users).
3. **Launch**: Q4 2025 (Global release).
