# Product Requirement Document (PRD): LuxeMarket

## 1. Executive Summary
**LuxeMarket** is a premium mobile marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of high-end, pre-owned luxury fashion. The application streamlines the consignment process with AI-driven authentication and offers buyers a seamless, trusted shopping experience. The visual language is minimalist, emphasizing high-quality imagery and white space, adhering to Google's Material 3 guidelines but customized for a luxury aesthetic.

## 2. Problem Statement
- **Trust Deficit**: High friction in the secondary luxury market due to counterfeit fears.
- **Cluttered UX**: Competitors utilize dense, overwhelming interfaces that detract from the "luxury" feel.
- **Inventory Stagnation**: Sellers struggle with complex listing flows.

## 3. User Personas
1.  **The Curator (Seller)**: High-net-worth individual clearing wardrobe. Values speed and concierge service. Pain point: tedious data entry.
2.  **The Hunter (Buyer)**: Aspirational fashion enthusiast looking for specific vintage pieces. Values search granularity and authenticity guarantees.

## 4. Core Features & Prioritization (MoSCoW)

### Must Have (P0)
- **Secure Auth**: Social Login (Google/Apple) + Email magic link.
- **Home Feed**: Personalized algorithmic feed of products with large imagery.
- **Smart Search**: Filtering by Brand, Condition, Price, Vintage.
- **Product Details (PDP)**: High-res zoom, conditioning reports, "Add to Cart".
- **Checkout**: Stripe integration, saved addresses, taxation calculation.

### Should Have (P1)
- **Visual Search**: Search by uploading an image.
- **Seller Dashboard**: Analytics on views/sales.

### Could Have (P2)
- **AR Try-On**: Virtual accessory placement.

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)
- **Conversion Rate**: Target >3.5% (industry avg 2%).
- **Time-to-List**: < 60 seconds for sellers.
- **Crash-Free Ratio**: >99.9%.

## 6. Rollout Plan
- **Phase 1 (Alpha)**: Internal dogfooding (Nov 15, 2025).
- **Phase 2 (Beta)**: Waitlist invite-only (Dec 1, 2025).
- **Phase 3 (Public)**: Global launch (Jan 10, 2026).
